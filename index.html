Raj Nagar Extension

    Locality introduction and neighbourhood:

     Situated adjacent to NH-34 and NH-9, Raj Nagar Extension is a developing residential
    locality in Ghaziabad,placed closer to the developed areas of Sanjay Nagar and Raj 
    Nagar. The locality is emerging as a residential destination due to its easy
    accessibility with Delhi, availability of affordable properties and sound. 
    

    Physical infrastructure:

     Raj Nagar Extension is connected with other key localities of Ghaziabad,Delhi and 
     Noida, via Meerut Road (NH-34), Meerut Bypass Road (NH-9) and Raj Nagar Extension 
     Road. It is 8 km from the nearest Shaheed Sthal (New Bus Adda) metro station placed 
     on the Red Line of Delhi Metro, which further extends the locality?s reach to 
     prominent areas of Delhi such as Kashmere Gate, Pitampura and Rohini.
Govindpuram:

   Locality introduction and neighbourhood:

   Located along the Hapur Road (NH-9) at the eastern edge of Ghaziabad,
   Govindpuram is one of the most affordable and developed locality. 
   Govindpuram?s residential footprint mostly consists of horizontal 
   developments with most of them being builder floors.
   The locality is surrounded by village area towards its north,
   east, and west direction.

   Social & retail infra:

   Despite the locality being some distance away from the centre of Ghiaziabad, Govindpuram has decent 
   social infrastructure catering to its residents. The locality and its nearby areas have a number of 
   schools, of which Ryan International School, Dehradun Public School are prominent. Shivalik Hospital,
   Maxwell Multi speciality Hospital and Columbia Asia Hospital are among the hospitals available to the
   residents here,
